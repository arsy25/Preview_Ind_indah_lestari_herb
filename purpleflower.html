Hibiscus Sabdariffa Egyptian Purple Flower Rosella Tea

Rosella Tea is made from selected natural purple hibiscus flowers (Hibiscus sabdariffa), carefully selected and sterilized with heat, then oven-dried at 130°C. This herbal tea provides a refreshing taste and is packed with natural antioxidants that support overall health and wellness.

Enjoyed both hot and cold, Rosella Tea has a tangy, fruity flavor and a rich red-purple color. It’s perfect for daily enjoyment and has been cherished for its traditional health benefits for generations.

Ingredients:

Natural dried hibiscus red flowers (Rosella), sugar

Manufacturing Process:

Carefully selected, heat sterilized, and oven-dried at 130°C.

Serving Suggestion:

Place 1 sachet (5g) of Rosella Tea into 300ml of boiling water and steep for 5 minutes. Add sugar (optional) for hot servings, or sugar/syrup for cold servings as preferred.
